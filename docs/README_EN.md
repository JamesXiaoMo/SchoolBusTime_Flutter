"SchoolBusTime" is an application designed for students at Fukuyama University in Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan, allowing them to check the real-time schedule of school buses. The inspiration came on a sunny Friday afternoon. Even though I was in my artificial intelligence class, my mind was already elsewhere, eager to head home. I opened the Fukuyama University website to check the departure time of the next bus. It was then that I realized how a simple query required several clicks on the website, which clearly violated the principles of user interaction design. Thus, the idea for "SchoolBusTime" was born.
